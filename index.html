ABOUT YOU


"Do you think I have forgotten about you?"




Solace

Who would've thought
that a girl like me,
who cherishes solitude,
would find herself lost
in your solace?







Harmony

In her laughter,
melodies softly croon,
My heart beats quickly,
under her sweet monsoo.
With every glance,
my affection has grown,
In her presence,
I've found my own true home.







Radiance

Her dazzling beauty sets
her apart amid a crowded room,
Much like the moon
stands out in the night sky.
Fascinated by her appealing charm,
A sight that leaves a lasting impression
on anyone who beholds it.







Glimmer

As I glanced across the room,
she caught my eye,
Every time our eyes met,
I felt so shy.
Her beauty shines
with a gentle glow,
My heart fluttered
as time moved slowly.







Enchantment

Her talents amaze me,
her voice is so sweet,
With every word,
my heart skips a beat.
Like a sunrise,
she brightens my day,
In her presence,I wish to stay.







Epiphany

Simple moments,
yet so profound,
In her, I found the word "love"
She is the force
that makes my world spin,
In admiration, she has truly captured my heart.
